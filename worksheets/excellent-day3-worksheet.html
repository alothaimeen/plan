Exercise 1: Past Progressive Tense

💡 Remember:
• Past Progressive: was/were + verb-ing
• Use: actions happening at a specific time in the past
• Example: I was studying when you called.

A. Complete with the Past Progressive:

1 At 8 PM yesterday, I (watch) a documentary about Saudi Arabia.
2 While King Abdulaziz (build) the country, many people supported him.
3 What you (do) at 9 AM this morning?
4 The students (not/sleep) when the teacher arrived.
5 it (rain) when you left home?

B. Past Progressive + When + Past Simple:

6 I (read) a book when my friend (call) me.
7 While we (travel) to Dubai, we (see) the Burj Khalifa.
8 The family (eat) dinner when a visitor (arrive).

Exercise 2: Writing with Capitals

📝 Capitals Rules:
1. Start of sentence
2. Names of people and places (cities, countries, rivers)
3. Days of the week and months
Example: King Abdulaziz established Riyadh on September 23, 1932.

Rewrite these sentences with correct capitals:

1 we visited dubai and abu dhabi last summer.
2 prince sultan bin salman flew on the space shuttle in june 1985.
3 the kingdom of saudi arabia was established in september.
4 my family is from jeddah, but we live in riyadh now.

Exercise 3: Write a Paragraph

Topic: "How the Internet Changed My Life"

📝 Paragraph Structure:
• Topic Sentence: Introduce your main idea
• Supporting Sentences: Give 3-4 examples or details
• Concluding Sentence: End with your opinion or summary
• Length: 60-80 words

Use these ideas if you need help:

  • Communication with friends and family
  • E-learning and online courses
  • Finding information quickly (Google)
  • Entertainment (YouTube, social media)
  • Online shopping

Exercise 4: Critical Thinking

Read and answer with YOUR opinion:

1 Is change always good? Why or why not?
2 What is the most important change in Saudi Arabia in your opinion?
3 How can students help make positive changes in their school or city?
4 Do you think technology makes life better or worse? Explain.
