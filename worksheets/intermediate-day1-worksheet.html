التمرين 1: تمييز الزمن

حدد الزمن المستخدم في كل جملة: Present Simple (PS) أو Present Continuous (PC)

1. I study English every day. _______
2. She is reading a book now. _______
3. They play football on Fridays. _______
4. We are watching TV at the moment. _______
5. He goes to school by bus. _______
6. I am doing my homework right now. _______
7. My mother cooks dinner every evening. _______
8. The students are writing a test now. _______
التمرين 2: إكمال الجمل

اختر الفعل الصحيح بين القوسين:

1. Ahmed usually                  (plays / is playing) football after school.

2. Look! The teacher                  (comes / is coming) to our class.

3. I                  (drink / am drinking) milk every morning.

4. Be quiet! The baby                  (sleeps / is sleeping) now.

5. My father                  (works / is working) in a hospital.

6. Listen! Someone                  (knocks / is knocking) on the door.

7. We always                  (eat / are eating) breakfast at 7 AM.

8. They                  (study / are studying) for the exam right now.

التمرين 4: نشاط المحادثة 🗣️

اعمل مع زميلك: اطرح الأسئلة وأجب عليها شفهياً

Student A: What do you do every day?

Student B: I study English and _______________.

Student A: What are you doing now?

Student B: I am _______________.

Student A: Do you play sports?

Student B: Yes, I _______________.

Student A: What is your friend doing right now?

Student B: He/She is _______________.
