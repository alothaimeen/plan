التمرين 1: اقرأ النص التالي

اقرأ النص بتركيز ثم أجب عن الأسئلة:

Big Changes in Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is experiencing big changes these days. The country is building new cities and modern buildings. Many young people are learning new skills and studying technology. Schools are using new teaching methods and computers. People are traveling more and seeing the world. Sports and entertainment are becoming more popular. Cinemas are opening in many cities. Women are working in different fields and driving cars. Life in Saudi Arabia is changing fast, and the future looks bright and exciting for everyone.

التمرين 2: أسئلة الفهم

اختر الإجابة الصحيحة:

1. What is happening in Saudi Arabia?

a) Nothing is changing ⃝
b) Big changes are happening ⃝
c) Only schools are changing ⃝

2. What are young people learning?

a) Old methods ⃝
b) New skills and technology ⃝
c) How to drive only ⃝

3. What is opening in many cities?

a) Schools ⃝
b) Hospitals ⃝
c) Cinemas ⃝

4. True or False: Women cannot work in Saudi Arabia now.

True ⃝     False ⃝

5. How does the text describe the future?

a) Dark and difficult ⃝
b) Bright and exciting ⃝
c) Same as before ⃝

التمرين 3: أسئلة مفتوحة

أجب عن الأسئلة بجمل كاملة:

1. What changes do you see in your school?

2. What new skill do you want to learn?

3. How is life different from 10 years ago?

التمرين 4: نشاط المحادثة 🗣️

اعمل مع زميلك: تبادل الأدوار واطرح الأسئلة

Student A: What changes do you see in our school?

Student B: I see _______________.

Student A: Do you like these changes? Why?

Student B: Yes/No, because _______________.

Student A: What changes do you want to see in the future?

Student B: I want _______________.

Student A: Are you learning any new skills?

Student B: Yes, I am learning _______________.
