التمرين 1: تمييز الزمن

حدد الزمن المستخدم في كل جملة: Present Simple (PS) أو Present Continuous (PC)

1. I study English every day. _______
2. She is reading a book now. _______
3. They play football on Fridays. _______
4. We are watching TV at the moment. _______
5. He goes to school by bus. _______
6. I am doing my homework right now. _______
7. My mother cooks dinner every evening. _______
8. The students are writing a test now. _______
التمرين 2: إكمال الجمل

اختر الفعل الصحيح بين القوسين:

1. Ahmed usually (plays / is playing) football after school.

2. Look! The teacher (comes / is coming) to our class.

3. I (drink / am drinking) milk every morning.

4. Be quiet! The baby (sleeps / is sleeping) now.

5. My father (works / is working) in a hospital.

6. Listen! Someone (knocks / is knocking) on the door.

7. We always (eat / are eating) breakfast at 7 AM.

8. They (study / are studying) for the exam right now.

التمرين 1: اقرأ النص التالي

اقرأ النص بتركيز ثم أجب عن الأسئلة:

Big Changes in Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is experiencing big changes these days. The country is building new cities and modern buildings. Many young people are learning new skills and studying technology. Schools are using new teaching methods and computers. People are traveling more and seeing the world. Sports and entertainment are becoming more popular. Cinemas are opening in many cities. Women are working in different fields and driving cars. Life in Saudi Arabia is changing fast, and the future looks bright and exciting for everyone.

True ⃝     False ⃝

التمرين 1: تحليل بنية الفقرة

اقرأ الفقرة التالية ثم حدد أجزاءها:

My name is Ahmed and I am a high school student. I study at Abi Bakr Al-Arabi School in Riyadh. I have many friends and we enjoy learning together. My favorite subject is English because it helps me communicate with people from different countries. I also like sports and play football every weekend. I hope to become an engineer in the future.

الآن حدد الأجزاء:

1. Topic Sentence (الجملة الرئيسية - أصفر):

2. Supporting Details (التفاصيل الداعمة - أخضر): كم جملة؟

3. Concluding Sentence (الجملة الختامية - أزرق):

التمرين 2: ترتيب الجمل

___ My family is very important to me.

___ We eat dinner together every evening and talk about our day.

___ I have two brothers and one sister.

___ My parents always support me in my studies.

___ We also travel together during vacations.

التمرين 3: اكتب فقرة منظمة

اكتب فقرة (60-80 كلمة) عن أحد المواضيع التالية:

✅ My Daily Routine (روتيني اليومي)

✅ Changes in My Life (التغييرات في حياتي)

✅ My Family (عائلتي)

تذكر:
  • ابدأ بجملة رئيسية واضحة (Topic Sentence)
  • أضف 4-5 جمل داعمة (Supporting Details)
  • اختم بجملة ختامية (Concluding Sentence)
Write your paragraph here...
التمرين 4: تحضير عرض قصير 🎤

  2. ضع خطاً تحت النقاط المهمة

  • انظر إلى زملائك
  • لا تقرأ مباشرة من الورقة
  • ابتسم وكن واثقاً

التمرين 2: استخدام أدوات الربط

اربط الجمل باستخدام الأدوات المناسبة: and, but, because, so

1. I like English. I study it every day.

→ I like English, I study it every day.

2. I want to play football. It is raining now.

→ I want to play football, it is raining now.

3. I study hard. I want to get good grades.

→ I study hard I want to get good grades.

4. I was tired. I went to bed early.

→ I was tired, I went to bed early.

5. I like reading. I like writing. I don't like math.

→ I like reading writing, I don't like math.
